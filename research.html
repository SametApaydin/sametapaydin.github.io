Research Projects

Securitization without Security: How Migration is Shaping the Global Order (SWS)
2025 – Present
Research Assistant, Mediterranean Team · Leir Institute for Migration and Human Security

Collecting, processing, and visualising data on irregular migration routes and nationalities to build spatial indicators and maps, combined with web-based monitoring of emerging trends and fieldwork with local stakeholders and authorities.

Decentring the Study of Migrant Returns and Return Policies (GAPs)
2024 – 2025
Consortium Member · Horizon Europe

Collected and systematised qualitative and quantitative data on return migration policies across EU member states and third countries, applying mixed methods to analyse patterns of return migration diplomacy and cooperation outcomes.

Measuring Irregular Migration (MirreM)
2023 – 2024
Country Expert, Türkiye · Horizon Europe

Analysed Türkiye's legal, policy, and institutional framework on irregular migration, assessed the production and limitations of irregular migration data, and prepared analytical country profiles and publishable country briefs.

Migration Governance and Asylum Crisis (MAGYC)
2020 – 2023
Consortium Member · Horizon Europe

Analysed Turkish parliamentary deliberations on migration, asylum, and refugee policies, tracing legislative priorities and political contestation over time and examining how domestic dynamics shaped Türkiye's external migration diplomacy.
