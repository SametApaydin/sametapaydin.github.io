The Long Rotation

Every sector as its own small chart — the chart pack's rotation panels (stories 10–11) made interactive. The line is the sector's contribution to Türkiye's dependence index; the colored strip beneath it shows who owned the risk each year. Where the strip changes color, the dependence changed hands. Click any panel to expand it.

    Reading the panels: a flat line with a changing strip is the chart pack's core finding in miniature — same amount of risk, new owner. A rising line with a stable strip is a deepening dependence on the same partner (the expensive kind). The rotation screener lists every handover with its year; the risk-shift screener ranks where the index is being built and unbuilt. Together they answer the two questions a minister actually asks: who owns our risk now, and since when?
