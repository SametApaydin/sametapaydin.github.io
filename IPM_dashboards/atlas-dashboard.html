Trade Explorer

An Atlas-of-Economic-Complexity-style interactive dashboard: pick a country, switch between exports and imports, break trade down by product or partner, and explore it as a treemap, time series, market-share chart, or map.

● All trade values are synthetic — generated deterministically in your browser. Country and product names are real; the numbers are not.

    About this demo: the layout algorithms (squarified treemap, stacked-area, tile cartogram), interaction model (drill-down, crosshair tooltips, isolate-on-legend-click, year animation), and control structure mirror the real Atlas explore page. Values are produced by a seeded random-number generator so each country has a stable, plausible-looking — but entirely fictional — trade profile that evolves smoothly over 1995–2025.
