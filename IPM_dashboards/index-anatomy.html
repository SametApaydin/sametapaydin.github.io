Index Anatomy

The dependence index taken apart as a sankey: Türkiye's total trade fans out into sectors, and each sector fans out into the partners who supply (or buy) it. Band thickness is money; band color is the sector's concentration — dark bands are where the index actually lives. This is Σ(sector share × sector HHI) drawn as plumbing, after resourcetrade.earth's sankey view.

Reading the anatomy: a fat, dark band from a sector to a single partner is the index made visible — lots of money, few alternatives. A fat but pale fan (one sector, many partners) is size without risk. The index-anatomy screener below is the accountant's view of the same picture: typically a handful of sector terms carry most of the aggregate number, which tells the policy team exactly where diversification effort pays.
