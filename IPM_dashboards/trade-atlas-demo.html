An Atlas-of-Economic-Complexity-style treemap and trend view, built from scratch to test how far the visualization layer alone gets you. Every number below is randomly generated — no country, product, or trade figure here is real.

● 100% synthetic data — generated in your browser, not fetched from anywhere
    What's real vs. simulated: the layout math (squarified treemap, stacked-area layering, hover/zoom interaction) is the same class of algorithm the Atlas uses. What's faked is the data itself — real bilateral trade data requires reconciling mismatched exporter/importer reports and harmonizing product codes across classification revisions (HS, SITC) over decades, which is a data-engineering and research problem, not a rendering one.
