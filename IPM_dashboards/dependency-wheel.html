Reading the wheel: Türkiye's arc (bottom) and each partner's arc are joined by a ribbon sized by trade value in the selected direction and sector. Color encodes dependence asymmetry: the same $10B corridor is a different risk depending on whether it is 2% of your trade and 30% of theirs — or the reverse. The squared logic of the HHI lives in the KPI row: the index shown is Σ(share²) over partners, so one thick ribbon moves it far more than five thin ones. Annotations on the timeline are illustrative, mirroring the real chart pack's events.
